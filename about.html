About Campus Connect

Campus Connect helps students discover events, join groups, collaborate on projects, and connect through shared interests.

Project Overview

  • Tech Stack: React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
  • Features: Groups, chat, events, interest matching
  • Security: College email verification & safe auth
